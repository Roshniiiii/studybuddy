Why study buddy?
Study buddy is an online platform where students in ASRJC can help each other with academics.
Students can either choose to ask a question regarding one of their subjects or they can choose to answer a question from the question database.
Study buddy creates an efficient environment for clarifying doubts or revising by answering questions.By having an online platform to learn, it saves times for students and makes learning easier.
